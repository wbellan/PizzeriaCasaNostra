Forza Forni Oven

As of Pizzeria Casa Nostra's opening, we are only 1 of 3 restaurants that feature the Bistro Twister by Forna Forzi. These historic Italian ovens are authentic with boutique style service.

The Bistro Twister features the world's only rotating monoblock refractory oven floor, which provides incredible even heat transfer and retention. Say goodbye to hot & cold spots, and enjoy the perfect bake.
